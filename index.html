Oaxaca, México.

Oaxaca de Juárez, the capital of the state of Oaxaca, is a colonial gem in southern Mexico, recognized as a World Heritage Site. It stands out for its Baroque architecture of green quarry stone, its unparalleled gastronomy, its vibrant indigenous traditions, and its proximity to archaeological sites such as Monte Albán. It is a cultural epicenter, famous for the Guelaguetza festival, mezcal, and the handicrafts of the Central Valleys.
